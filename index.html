Manifesto

Our Goal

Minecuna's primary goal is to achieve Creative Mode in Survival on a Vanilla world.

Specifically this relates to the automation and saving of *all* resources, where possible, to avoid the need to grind for resources when building something. This philosophy specifically addresses our time being the most valuable resource.

This allows us to achieve our secondary goal of building structures on an epic scale, a scale judged by, but not limited to, the ability to see structures from Orbit.

Community

Minecuna has always been a place for friends to socialise and a central part of this is the community comes before the game. We're a group of friends socialising, Minecraft is simply a medium.

In-game and elsewhere (IRC, GitHub, etc) we aim to maintain a friendly and respectful atmosphere that is tolerant of people's creativity, whatever form that takes and is respectful of skill level.

We are also child-friendly, that is to say, many players have children who join in from time to time so language and topic needs to be adjusted for a younger audience when necessary.
